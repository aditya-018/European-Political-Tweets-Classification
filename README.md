## European-Political-Tweets-Classification

Twitter has been one of the most commonly used
online social media platforms to express your insights and
concerns to the general public. While some of these do not
go well with a few sections of the people and go well with
other sections. The project we have worked on is quite similar
to this, where a group of leaders and Twitter users from
the Scandinavian countries. With two datasets training and
testing with approximately 510,000 tweets total, we predicted
the political views that the owner of the tweet has tweeted.
